Introduction

This application is part of the project 'Ontologies for epistemic uncertainties'. The project was conducted as a dissertation thesis for the MSc Data Science degree at Lancaster University. The student is Vasileios Vyzas under the supervision of Keith Beven. The aim of this application is to provide a guide for modellers in environmental science to help capture their knowledge about a specific analysis they want to perform and to suggest some tools that might be appropriate for their analysis.
